Logo

"The logo for "The Fix-It Shop" expertly employs the principles of composition in graphic design. Symmetry and balance are achieved through the overlapping of two tools, creating a sense of stability and order. The proximity of the tools emphasizes unity and cohesion. Contrasting colors, black and orange, create visual interest and draw attention. The emphasis on the tools, through size and color, establishes a clear visual hierarchy. Movement and energy are conveyed through the overlapping tools. The circular shape, formed by the tools, represents unity and wholeness. The logo's limited color palette and simple shapes create a sense of cohesion and visual flow, achieving unity. Effective use of negative space creates clarity and balance. Overall, the logo effectively utilizes the principles of composition to convey "The Fix-It Shop" mission and values, establishing a strong visual identity for the brand. " by A. Mendoza
